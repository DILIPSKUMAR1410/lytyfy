1.4 billion people in the world have no access to power and 400 million of them live in rural India. Lytyfy helps in providing quality solar energy solutions to low income rural families at their doorsteps.
The major stumbling block in alleviating energy poverty is lack of access to finance and with Lytyfy we are determined to change that.
